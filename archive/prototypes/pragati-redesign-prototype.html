Our story

আমাদের লক্ষ্য, আমাদের স্বপ্ন A community built on culture, compassion, and continuity.

"We strive to support educational and healthcare initiatives, and make a sense of welcome to people from all backgrounds."

Formed in 2012, Pragati is one of the oldest Bengali organizations on the East Coast — dedicated to promoting unity and cultural enrichment within the Greater Philadelphia Bengali community. We continue to honor our heritage while extending warm welcome to all who wish to participate.
